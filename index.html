INTRODUCING HYPERDRAWv2! Faster, Smarter, Better 😎

HyperDraw_v2 remains available as Model 7. New Alg 45 family builds are now in Models 45 and 52–58.

Old model: HyperDraw used a lighter stroke classifier that sampled fewer points and matched against a smaller pattern bank. It was solid, but struggled with messy lines and uncommon shapes.

New model: HyperDraw_v2 runs a deeper shape pass with better stroke grouping, cleaner confidence scoring, and a wider pattern memory. That makes it quicker to lock in a result and more accurate on rough sketches.
